CATEGORIES

Footwear

Footwear are versatile and comfortable, ideal for everyday wear. They are usually made of materials such as leather, suede, canvas or synthetic textiles, combining style and functionality. They usually have rubber soles or flexible materials that provide good grip and cushioning.

Fitness equipment

Fitness centers usually occupy large spaces, but the space can also be medium or small. It all depends on the amenities and activities they offer. Some have indoor and outdoor sports facilities like swimming pools, running tracks, rock climbing, sports playing fields, racquetball courts, rooms for martial arts, yoga, Pilates, spinning, weight training as classes or for individual use, and regular gym equipment such as treadmills, elliptical and cycling machines. They may also include saunas, hot tubs, steam rooms, tanning beds, warm-up and cool rooms, showers, locker rooms, childcare services, juice bars, snack bars, and retail sports stores.

Outdoor sports

Outdoor sports are physical activities that are practiced in an open space and that have a connection with nature. It can include a variety of activities, from hiking, rock climbing, and camping to water sports such as kayaking and surfing. Outdoor sports offer many benefits, including improved physical fitness, the opportunity to relieve stress, and the chance to connect with nature. .
